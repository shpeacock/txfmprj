Our Purpose

The Texas Family Project is a non-profit service organization promoting progressive family values. We work with progressive state and local campaigns that are committed to protecting Texas families of all types from intolerance and discrimination. We believe that all Texans have the r ight to live their lives free from hate and inequality in safe communities, schools, and workplaces.

Copyright © by Peacock Web Productions

The purposes for which the corporation is organized are to promote social welfare within the meaning of section 501(c)(4) of the Internal Revenue Code, including but not limited to: (1) developing and advocating for legislation, regulations, and government programs to protect family values, improve the environment, protect natural resources, and stimulate the economy, and (2) conducting research and publicizing the positions of elected officials concerning these issues.
