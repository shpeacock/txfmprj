Our Purpose

The Texas Family Project is a non-profit service organization promoting progressive family values. We work with progressive state and local campaigns that are committed to protecting Texas families of all types from intolerance and discrimination. We believe that all Texans have the r ight to live their lives free from hate and inequality in safe communities, schools, and workplaces.
