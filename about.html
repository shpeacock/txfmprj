Our Mission

Healthcare Rights

All Texas families deserve affordable world class healthcare

Safe Communities

We support the development of safe communities for all Texas families

Civil Rights

All Texas families deserve equal rights and fair treatment under the law

Economic Stimulus

Our economy will flourish through targeted public spending and a strong workforce

Preserve and protect the environment

Texas is a beautiful state and we will work to preserve it for our children

Protect the marginalized

All Texas families must be treated equally, no matter the background, gender or orientation of their members

Improve and Protect Family Social Services

We have a responsibility to protect our most vulnerable family members

Support and Improve Public Education

Texas needs a strong public education system with safe schools and world-class teachers

Veterans Affairs

Our military service members and those who have faithfully served must receive the care they deserve.
